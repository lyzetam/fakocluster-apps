New Time-Series Tables

oura_sleep_phase_timeseries

Granularity: Sleep stages at 5-minute and 30-second intervals
Data Captured:
• sleep_phase_5_min: Encoded sleep stages per 5 minutes
• sleep_phase_30_sec: Encoded sleep stages per 30 seconds
• movement_30_sec: Movement detection per 30 seconds
Use Case: Analyze sleep architecture, detect disruptions, study sleep cycles

Granularity: Activity classification and MET values throughout the day
Data Captured:
• class_5_min: Activity intensity per 5-minute interval (rest, low, medium, high)
• met_items: Metabolic equivalent values with timestamps
Use Case: Track activity patterns, identify peak intensity times, analyze daily movement distribution
